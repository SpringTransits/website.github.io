How does this work

Screening process:

We build a Pod first, which is made up of aspirants with following attributes:

  1. Tech Background: We are hoping that majority of Pod members have STEM background.
  1. Time Commitment: at minimum 2 hours and maximum 5 hours of time commitment is required per day. We have come up with the time commitment based on the time a new mother can spare to reboot a rewarding tech carrier in 5 years.
  1. Past experience: We prefer members to have some experience, we want to make sure that a Pod as a whole entity has at least 1 year of average work experience.
  1. Role fitment: We have limited roles that make up a tech company, Dev, AS, TPM and PM. We will expand as we grow but for now, we actively look for members who want to fit into one of these roles.
  1. Alignment with current programs/products/services: We are a Microsoft tech stack based company to begin with, so we will be looking for individuals who are looking to make a carrier in Microsoft stack. As we develop more partnerships and reach we will definitely expand to Open Source, Google, Amazon and any other tool chain.
  1. Contribution potential: We are really looking for people who are serious about getting into workforce in future and are willing to give time and energy. We might not be interested in someone who want to get into a job in next 6 months to a year.
  1. Team dynamics: We are very particular about who becomes part of the team, team as a whole will decide who could join when they are backfilling an open spot.
  1. Pod allotment
    1. Unless there is a room in a group (pod) to work with, we will not absorb new hires. We are intentionally keeping the Pod size to 5 or 6 individuals.
    1. As these PODs are self-reliant and close-knit, its important to look at the team dynamics and fitment, both location and alignment with POD timings.
  1. Initial 10-week program: After initial screening, individual would spend 10 weeks to acclimatize and ramp with the team. There will be an evaluation by the team on various factors including technical competency, role, time commitment, complementary skills, and ST values rubric.
  1. POD voting and finalize selection for contract to hire role.

Products and Services:

We will focus on B2B + SaaS + IoT Wireless + Data Analytics + AI based products and services as a mainstay for the company. However, we will work on any project that will help build technical depth and is related to Microsoft stack.

Pay Model:

Revenue sharing: We are going to pay pro-bono and also share a percentage of revenue.
